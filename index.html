Program Sections

With nine junior program, three professional program, a mixed side, a range of teenager program, various touring sides and Back to Hockey sessions, Hockey's Club offers hockey to players of any ability.

Junior Program

The Junior program provides a welcoming and social place to learn, have fun, make friends, and enjoy membership.

Teenager Program

Hockey's Club teenager program aims to engage, challenge and inspire players of all abilities for them to achieve to the best of their ability or aspirations.

Professional Program

Hockey's Club has 10 professional teams for playing in competitive leagues. The section encourages juniors to play professional hockey when they are ready.
